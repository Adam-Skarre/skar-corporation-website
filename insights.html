Our research standard

From question to defensible recommendation.

Strong insight is not a collection of charts. It is a documented chain from the original question through assumptions, evidence, analysis, and the limits of what can be concluded.

01

Frame

Define the decision, constraints, stakeholders, and the evidence required.

02

Model

Build a transparent analytical structure and test the most important assumptions.

03

Challenge

Compare scenarios, quantify sensitivity, and identify where uncertainty changes the answer.

04

Communicate

Translate the work into a recommendation that decision-makers can understand and use.
