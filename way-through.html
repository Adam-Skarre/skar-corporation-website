Why this series exists

The difficult part is rarely seeing the mountain.
It is finding the pass.

In Norwegian, a skar is a notch or cleft in a mountain ridge—a narrow, often passable opening through difficult terrain. The Way Through examines the moments when progress depends on judgment: seeing the constraint clearly, comparing imperfect options, and choosing a practical route.
