Skar Corporation is a founder-led consulting practice built to help organizations approach difficult decisions with clarity, discipline, and practical judgment.

A founder-led consulting practice.

Skar Corporation works across engineering and business operations. Technology, software, and AI are tools we apply when they improve the result. We help clients frame complex problems, analyze evidence, and select practical next steps.

The company is led by Adam Skarre, whose project experience spans engineering analysis, quantitative modeling, energy systems, infrastructure strategy, and software-based decision tools.

Our standard is simple: be honest about the problem, rigorous about the analysis, and useful in the final recommendation.
